Following you will find a number of the best books to learn more about statistics and its philosophy. 

* [Opinionated Lessons on Statistics](http://wpressutexas.net/coursewiki/index.php/OpinionatedLessons.org/)
* [Introduction to Statistical Learning](http://www-bcf.usc.edu/~gareth/ISL/)
* [The Elements of Statistical Learning](https://web.stanford.edu/~hastie/ElemStatLearn/)
* [Applied Predicitive Modeling](http://appliedpredictivemodeling.com/)
* [Statistical Inference](https://www.amazon.com/Statistical-Inference-George-Casella/dp/0534243126)
* [Statistical Rethinking](http://xcelab.net/rm/statistical-rethinking/)
* [Data Analysis Using Regression and Multilevel/Hierarchical Models](http://www.stat.columbia.edu/~gelman/arm/)
* [Mostly Harmless Econometrics](https://press.princeton.edu/titles/8769.html)
* [Mastering Metrics: The Path from Cause to Effect](https://press.princeton.edu/titles/10363.html)
* [All of Statistics](https://www.amazon.com/All-Statistics-Statistical-Inference-Springer/dp/1441923225)
* [Statistics](https://www.amazon.com/Statistics-Third-David-Freedman/dp/0393970833/ref=sr_1_1?s=books&ie=UTF8&qid=1507057240&sr=1-1&keywords=statistics+freedman+3rd+e)
* [Statistics for Experimenters](https://www.amazon.com/dp/0471718130/ref=cm_sw_r_cp_ep_dp_Xja1zb9FANZ71)
* [Think Bayes](http://greenteapress.com/wp/think-bayes/)
* [Computer Age Statistical Inference](https://web.stanford.edu/~hastie/CASI/)
* [Think Stats](http://greenteapress.com/wp/think-stats-2e/)
* [Machine Learning for Hackers](http://shop.oreilly.com/product/0636920018483.do)
* [Probability and Statistics](http://mplab.ucsd.edu/tutorials/ProbabilityAndStats.pdf)
* [Statistical Evidence: A likelihood paradigm](https://www.amazon.com/Statistical-Evidence-Likelihood-Monographs-Probability/dp/0412044110)
